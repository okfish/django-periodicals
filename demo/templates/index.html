django-periodicals

This is the demonstration site for django-periodicals. A Django application for periodical/magazine websites with fully cross linked indices. There are list and detail views for: Periodical, Issue, Article, Author, Article Series and Tags. The application provides full text search of article titles and descriptions. A complete set of templates are provided. A sitemap is also dynamically generated.

This demonstration shows how the templates can be easily customized and styled. Bootstrap isn't required/used by the default templates.
